# Advanced Topics in AWS Route 53

1. **Health Checks**
   - Monitor endpoints and automatically route traffic away from unhealthy resources.

2. **Weighted Routing**
   - Distribute traffic across multiple resources based on weights.

3. **Geolocation Routing**
   - Serve users from a region closest to their location.

4. **Failover Routing**
   - Automatically switch traffic to standby resources during failure.

5. **DNSSEC (Domain Name System Security Extensions)**
   - Adds integrity and authenticity to DNS data.

6. **Integration with CloudFront & ELB**
   - Alias records simplify pointing your domain to AWS services.

7. **Private Hosted Zones**
   - Manage internal DNS for resources within VPCs.
