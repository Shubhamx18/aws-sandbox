# Types of DNS Records in Route 53

1. **A Record**
   - Maps a domain name to an IPv4 address.

2. **AAAA Record**
   - Maps a domain name to an IPv6 address.

3. **CNAME Record**
   - Maps a domain name to another domain name.

4. **MX Record**
   - Directs email to mail servers.

5. **TXT Record**
   - Store text data, often for verification or SPF/DKIM.

6. **NS Record**
   - Delegates a domain or subdomain to another DNS server.

7. **Alias Record**
   - AWS-specific, maps domain to AWS resources like S3 or ELB without extra cost.

8. **SRV Record**
   - Service record, used for service discovery in networks.
