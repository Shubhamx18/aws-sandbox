Benefits of Amazon RDS:

1. Fully Managed: AWS handles maintenance, patching, backups.
2. High Availability: Multi-AZ ensures uptime and failover.
3. Scalability: Scale storage and compute resources as needed.
4. Automated Backups: Enables point-in-time recovery.
5. Security: Encryption at rest/in transit, VPC isolation.
6. Monitoring: CloudWatch metrics and alerts.
7. Reduced Operational Overhead: Focus on apps instead of DB.
